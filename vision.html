Our Vision for Somalia’s Future

To build a strong, united Somalia anchored on centralized command and people-driven governance.

To rise defense capabilities that protect sovereignty and peace.

To eliminate political instability by empowering citizens and promoting transparency.

To foster economic growth and social development that benefits every Somali family.

To build partnerships locally and internationally, inspired by leadership models from countries like China, Turkey, and Russia.

To uphold national pride by learning from our heroes and global leaders.

Himiladeena Mustaqbalka Soomaaliya

Inaad dhisto Soomaaliya xoogan oo mideysan, iyadoo laga dhisay amarka dhexe iyo hogaamin dadweyne.

Kor u qaadida awoodda difaaca si loo ilaaliyo madaxbannaanida iyo nabadda.

Ka takhalusidda xasilooni darada siyaasadeed adoo awood siinaya muwaadiniinta iyo daahfurnaanta.

Kobcinta dhaqaalaha iyo horumarinta bulshada ee faa’iido u leh qoys kasta oo Soomaaliyeed.

Dhisidda iskaashiyo gudaha iyo dibadda, oo ku dayasho leh tusaalooyinka hoggaamiyeyaasha sida Shiinaha, Turkiga, iyo Ruushka.

Xushmeynta qarannimada iyada oo laga baranayo halyeeyadeena iyo hoggaamiyeyaasha caalamka.
