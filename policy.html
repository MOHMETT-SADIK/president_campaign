The Rise of a Nation: Presidential Statement by Mohamed

A New Dawn for Somalia Begins Now.

Somalia has endured decades of chaos, foreign interference, broken institutions, and wounded pride. But the age of disorder ends here. Under my leadership, a new force rises — built not on empty promises, but on discipline, centralized strength, and the will of the people.

I do not lead for power. I lead for restoration — of sovereignty, unity, confidence. A Somalia where borders are secure, the economy is stable, and citizens are proud to be Somali.

A Commanded Vision for a Powerful Somalia

My governance is rooted in centralized command — not scattered authority, but disciplined focus. Inspired by the courage of Mohamed Siad Barre and the boldness of Donald Trump, I envision a leadership that builds before crisis, acts before collapse.

People-Driven, Order-Based Leadership

The people are Somalia’s energy. My policies reflect your voice — gathered through public forums and citizen consultations. But unity requires structure. A nation must be led. A ship cannot sail if every hand tries to steer.

Defense and Food Security as Sacred Duties

No nation survives without defense. Our military — land, sea, and air — will be modern, Somali-built, and respected. Neutrality does not mean weakness. If Somalia is provoked, we will respond with power. Food sovereignty will also be sacred — no Somali shall starve in silence.

Foreign Policy with Dignity

We will partner with China, Turkey, Russia — not as dependents, but equals. No more puppet regimes. We will create policies of strength, not charity. Reforms will be real — Somali-driven, not donor-mandated.

A Legacy of True Revival

This is not a campaign — it’s a national rebirth. My legacy will be carved in infrastructure, independence, and security. Somalia will not be pitied. Somalia will be praised.

Soo Kacitaan Qaran: Hadalka Madaxweynaha Maxamed

Waagu Wuu Dilaacay — Soomaaliya Wuu Iftinayaa.

Soomaaliya waxay martay geeddi dheer oo qadhaadh — burbur, faragelin shisheeye, xukuumado daciif ah, iyo qarannimo dhaawacantay. Laakiin waayihii mugdiga wuu dhamaaday. Hogaamintaydu waxay astaan u tahay kacdoon dhab ah — mid ku dhisan nidaam, xoog dhexe, iyo rabitaanka shacabka.

Ma doonayo awood, waxaan doonayaa soo celin — madax-bannaani, midnimo, kalsooni. Soomaaliya leh xuduudo la ilaaliyo, dhaqaale xasiloon, iyo dad jecel inay Soomaali yihiin.

Himilada Hoggaamin Xoog Leh

Waxaan aaminsanahay in Soomaaliya u baahantahay hoggaan xooggan oo hal meel ka amar qaata. Waan diidanahay kala-firidhsanaanta. Waxaan ku daydaa geesinimadii Siyaad Barre iyo siyaasaddii adag ee Trump. Ma sugayo dhibaato — waxaan u dhisayaa kahor burbur.

Shacab Awood Leh — Hoggaan Nidaamsan

Shacabka waa xooggayga. Siyaasad kastaa waxay ka dhalatay codkiinna. Waxaan fursad siinaa shirka shacabka iyo la-tashiga. Laakiin sida doonta aysan u socon karin iyadoo dadkoo dhami wataan, dalkuna ma socon karo haddi aanan la hoggaamin.

Difaaca Qaranka iyo Cunto Sugnaanta

Difaacu ma ahan ikhtiyaar. Waa waajib qaran. Ciidankeenu wuxuu noqon doonaa mid casri ah, Somali ku dhisan, ciidan lagu ixtiraamo Afrika iyo caalamka. Nabadduna ma ahan tabar-darro. Haddii la ina weeraro — waan ka aar-gudan doonaa. Qof Soomaali ah oo gaajeysan — lama aqbalo.

Siyaasad Dibadeed Oo Sharaf Ku Dhisan

Waan la macaamili doonaa waddamada sida Shiinaha, Ruushka, iyo Turkiga — laakiin ma ahaan doono dal amaah raadinaya. Waxaan nahay Soomaali leh xorriyad iyo sharaftooda. Isbeddelka naga yimaada, maaha inoo laga qoro.

Dhaxalka Dib-U-Dhiska Qaranka

Taariikhdu ma xusi doonto hadalkeyga — waxay xusi doontaa waddooyin, askar, dugsiyo, xuduudo la ilaaliyo. Soomaaliya ma noqon doonto dal caawimaad dalbada. Waxa ay noqon doontaa dal caawimaad bixiya.

Kuwa maqla codkayga: ma tihidin dad la iloobay, la liiday, ama la kala qaybiyey. Tani waa dalkiinna. Waa waqtigiinna.

Soomaaliya waa kacday — mana dhici doonto mar kale.
